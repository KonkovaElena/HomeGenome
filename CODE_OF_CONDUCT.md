# Code Of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository discusses sensitive topics: genomics, privacy, biosecurity, AI interpretation and therapy-adjacent futures.

Discussion must stay respectful, evidence-based and scoped to the project.

## Expected Behavior

1. critique claims, evidence and architecture rather than people;
2. stay precise and calm in technical disagreement;
3. avoid hype, intimidation or personal attacks;
4. avoid posting secrets, private medical data or sensitive biological material;
5. keep outreach and debate grounded in what the repository actually documents.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. harassment, threats or discriminatory language;
2. posting real genomes, VCFs, clinical reports or identifying biological data;
3. publishing exploit details or sensitive misuse paths in public threads;
4. spam, bad-faith derailment or repeated off-scope promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove comments and contributions that harm contributor safety, publication safety or the repository's technical quality.
